What are your prices?

You can see all of our packages/prices on the 'Packages' page. You can contact us if you want more specific packages, and we will make a personal package for you.

How do i order?

You can order by clicking on the package you want. It is also possible to order by contacting us by email or phone.

Do you work internationally?

We are currently only accepting orders from Belgium and the Netherlands. It is possible that we will expand in the next couple of years.

What should I wear?

Its your day so wear what makes you feel special. It is also possible to wear multiple outfits in 1 shoot.

Can I choose the photographer?

We are currently a team of 3 photographers. Every photographer is specialized in its package. this means that you can't choose the photographer. Don't worry tho, all the photographers have a heart of gold.

Can I cancel my order?

It is possible to request a cancel for your order if:

  1. the fotoshoot cancel request is minimum 2 weeks before the meeting
  2. you have not yet received the album
  3. you have called and send a email on the contact page

THE DEPOSIT WILL NOT BE REFUNDED IF YOU CANCELED LESS THAN 1 WEEK IN ADVANCED!
